On the Subject of Space Traders

You are no longer a bomb diffuser. You are now a SPACE TRADER!

The module is a star map with hypercorridors marked on it. At the bottom left, the maximum tax amount per vessel is displayed, as well as the number of products sold and the number of products to be sold.

When you select a star, you will receive information about its name, the race that lives there, the political regime and the tax amount. Using star's name, race and regime, determine if you should pay tax or not using the following tables. Note: there is no need to pay tax in the solar system (Sun).

The best selling prices are in the outskirts, so you only need to sell there. Outskirts are the star systems that has only one hypercorridor. To send a merchant ship to the star system press it. But be careful: if on the way from the solar system to the destination (including the end point) the tax amount is greater than the maximum tax amount per vessel, then strike will be occured. When there is strike on the bomb (caused by this or any other module) the number of sold products will be reseted, new number of products to sold, maximum tax amount and star systems' political regime will be generate. As soon as the required number of products is sold, the module will be solved.
